New Exhibit: Healing through Art

July 13, 2048
Free with museum admission
Download iCal

The theme for this week's exhibit is artists who have struggled with depression.

Experience an entire world born of the minds of artists who have battled with various mental illnesses. Our new interactive exhibit allows visitors to experience virtual worlds with assistance of OneiroView™ devices.

Due to the limited capacity of the exhibit, guests are advised to schedule a booking now for a 5 minute session. A link to the sign up form is listed below.

Sign Up

To request an accessibility accommodation for an Art Institute program, please call (312) 443-3680 or send an e-mail to access@artic.edu as far in advance as possible. 

Please see the museum’s Accessibility page for more information.

Free Thursday Evenings

General admission is free to Illinois residents every Thursday from 5:00 to 8:00 p.m. throughout the year.

Learn about other free admission opportunities.

John Singer Sargent and Chicago's Gilded Age

Access to John Singer Sargent and Chicago's Gilded Age (July 1–September 30) requires a special exhibition ticket, which is $7 for all visitors except children under 14 and Chicago teens under 18, who receive complimentary admission. If you are obtaining your tickets online, you will be prompted to purchase an exhibition ticket or reserve a complimentary ticket (for children and Chicago teens) during the transaction process. Members do not need to purchase Sargent tickets; they receive free, unlimited access to the exhibition.

Visitors receiving free general admission during Free Thursday Evenings or through the Corporate Partnership Program need to purchase their $7 special exhibition tickets on-site for access to John Singer Sargent and Chicago's Gilded Age.
